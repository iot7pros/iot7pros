Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use in your sculptures?

I primarily work with brass, copper, and electronic components. Each sculpture is carefully crafted using a combination of traditional metalworking techniques and modern electronic elements to create unique, functional art pieces.

Do you accept custom commissions?

Yes, I do accept custom commissions for both sculptures and electronic projects. Each piece is uniquely designed to meet your specific requirements while maintaining my signature aesthetic and technical standards.

How long does it take to complete a sculpture?

The timeframe varies depending on the complexity of the piece. Typically, a sculpture takes 4-6 weeks from concept to completion. Custom projects may take longer based on specific requirements and current workload.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship worldwide. Each piece is carefully packaged to ensure safe delivery. Shipping costs and timeframes vary by location. Please contact me for specific shipping details to your region.

Are your sculptures interactive?

Many of my sculptures feature interactive elements through LEDs, sensors, or other electronic components. Each piece is designed to create a unique experience while maintaining functionality and artistic integrity.

About Me

Hi, I'm 3Dpros iOT. I create sculptural electronic objects that celebrate both form and function. My work explores the intersection of art and technology, where circuits become visual elements and electronic components transform into artistic media.

Each piece is carefully crafted using brass rods, electronic components, and various materials, creating three-dimensional circuit sculptures that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. The exposed nature of these circuits allows viewers to appreciate the beauty of electronic design while understanding its functionality.

When I'm not building sculptures, I'm experimenting with new techniques, teaching workshops, or collaborating with other artists in the electronic art community.
